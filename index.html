2January

Breakfast and lunch included

5:30 AM-Depart Hotel (breakfast to go on bus)

9AM-Arrive in Assin Manso Ancestral Slave River Park .

This was one of the largest slave markets for gathering people to sell into slavery during the infamous trans-Atlantic slave trade. Assin Manso served as the place where slaves had their last bath on their journey.

10:30AM-Depart Assin Manso

11:30AM- Arrive at Cape Coast Slave Dungeon .
This is where slaves were held before they were loaded onto ships and taken to America to be sold . This "gate of no return" was the last stop before crossing the Atlantic Ocean. Given this will be a very emotional and thought provoking experience, we will have a debrief session over lunch to discuss and process the atrocities of those events.

1 PM-Depart Cape Coast Dungeon

1:30 PM-Lunch and debrief of Tour (included)
